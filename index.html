How to Use This Website

Welcome to our Digital SAT Practice Platform! Here's a quick guide to help you get started:

  1. Question Display:
    • Each question is sourced from the official Educator Question Bank.
    • Questions are displayed with their unique Question ID for easy reference.
  2. Stopwatch:
    • Use the stopwatch to time your responses and practice managing your time per question.
    • Click the gear icon next to the stopwatch to start, stop, or reset the timer.
  3. Question Navigation:
    • Click the "Next" button to move to the next question.
    • Questions continue indefinitely, allowing for unlimited practice.
  4. Question Counter:
    • Keep track of how many questions you've completed.
    • Use this to monitor your progress and set practice goals.
  5. Answer Checking:
    • Select your answer and click "Submit" to check if it's correct.
    • Receive immediate feedback and an explanation to enhance your learning.

Feel free to explore and practice at your own pace. Happy studying!
